About Us

we are glad to see you checking out our website. kast is simply an acronym for my roommate and i's names (kenny, ayush, steven and thomas). we started this organization because we wanted people to have a safe space to enjoy meeting other people their age at ordained venues. frat row and atlanta clubs are very sharp ends of the spectrum so we worked hard to create a space that feels very open to everyone. from scad/gsu artsy kids to the more reserved and nerdy emory and tech kids - we have them all. our parties range from 350-700 partipants every time so you are sure to connect with others and have a great time. again, we appreciate all of you for taking time out of your day to join us.
